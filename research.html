Research & Publications

We explore cutting-edge topics in Artificial Intelligence.

Research Areas

Computer Vision

Our computer vision research focuses on understanding dynamic scenes from video data. We are developing novel architectures for video object segmentation, action recognition, and 3D reconstruction.

Robotics & Control

We work on bridging the gap between simulation and reality for robotic manipulation. Key projects include dexterous hand manipulation and navigation in unstructured environments.

Efficient Deep Learning

Aiming to deploy AI on edge devices, we investigate model compression, quantization, and neural architecture search to create efficient yet powerful models.
